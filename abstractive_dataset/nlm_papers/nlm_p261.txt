Listen to this podcast as experts discuss 2 patient cases focusing on diagnosing 
and treating co-occurring insomnia and mental illness. The first case features a 
32-year-old man with bipolar disorder who is experiencing psychiatric symptoms 
and poor sleep, and the second case involves a 27-year-old man with 
schizophrenia who is having auditory hallucinations as well as chronic issues 
with insomnia.
